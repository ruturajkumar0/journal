Publication Ethics

The International Journal of Pharmacology Research follows international ethical publishing standards.

Authors Responsibilities

  • Original Work
  • No Plagiarism
  • No Duplicate Submission
  • Accurate Data Reporting

Editors Responsibilities

  • Fair Review Process
  • Confidentiality
  • Conflict of Interest Management

Reviewers Responsibilities

  • Objective Evaluation
  • Confidential Review
  • Ethical Recommendations
