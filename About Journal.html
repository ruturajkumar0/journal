About The Journal

International Journal of Pharmacology Research (IJPR)

International Journal of Pharmacology Research (IJPR) is an international, peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research in pharmacology, pharmaceutical sciences, clinical pharmacy, drug discovery, toxicology, pharmacovigilance, biotechnology, and related healthcare disciplines.

The journal provides a platform for researchers, academicians, clinicians, and industry professionals to share innovative findings, systematic reviews, case studies, and emerging developments in the field of pharmacological sciences.

Vision

To become a globally recognized journal promoting excellence in pharmacological research and scientific innovation.

Mission

To disseminate reliable, ethical, and impactful scientific knowledge through rigorous peer review and open access publishing.

Publisher

Lakshmi Biosciences

Publication Frequency

Quarterly (4 Issues Per Year)

Language

English

Open Access Policy

All articles published in IJPR are freely accessible immediately upon publication, ensuring maximum visibility and global dissemination of scientific knowledge.
